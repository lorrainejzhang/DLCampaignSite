Dave Singh

I'm a junior in Mechanical & Biomedical Engineering. I'm running to be your Student Body President because I want to help lead Carnegie Mellon to a brighter future and I hope you can help me get there. Most of what I do and what I've done is to further my goal of helping people wherever I am. I'm in MechE/BME because I want to help people who have medical impairments regain their ability to move. I'm an RA in Hamerschlag because I want to help first-years find their passions, pursue their dreams, be caught up with all the resources CMU has to offer, and feel comfortable in their living space. I am a CMU student because I want to make my mark on the world and there's no place I'd rather learn how. As SBP, as an RA, as a CMU student, and as a human being, I will represent you and your interests, no matter what community or background you come from. You are all valid. You all deserve to have your thoughts and beliefs heard.

Lorraine Zhang
